Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Murat Keskin | Portfolio" mobile application ("the App") handles information. This App is a personal portfolio showcase and does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. There is no account creation, no analytics tracking, and no advertising SDKs included in the App.

Data Stored on Your Device

All content displayed in the App (projects, skills, contact information) is bundled with the App itself and does not change based on user input. The App does not write any personal data to your device's storage.

Internet Access & External Links

The App requests internet access only to open external links that you explicitly tap, such as:

  • Project GitHub repositories and live demo links
  • Social media profiles (GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
  • Sending an email via your device's mail app

These links open in your device's browser or relevant app, which are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
